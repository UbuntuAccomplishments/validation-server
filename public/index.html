Earn trophies to showcase your experience with Ubuntu and the Community

Ubuntu Accomplishments runs in the background on your computer(s) discovering your accomplishments as you use your computer and engage with the Ubuntu Community.

Accomplishments

The Ubuntu Accomplishments viewer app shows the trophies you have accomplished.

Opportunities

The Ubuntu Accomplishments viewer app also lets you discover new opportunities to contribute to the Community or expand your experience with Ubuntu.

Trophy Details

Each trophy has instructions on how to achieve the accomplishment and where you can seek out further advice along with useful tips and common mistakes (pitfalls).
